About

Hi, I'm Eze! Welcome to my domain!

If I had one superpower, it would be the ability to learn quickly. Let me explain. I believe that personality traits and intelligence can be developed. And people grow through application and experience. The only thing between my current self and the best version of myself I want to become is learning through experience. So it follows that learning quickly would get me there faster. It's a good thing that this 'superpower' isn't exclusive to superheroes.

My mission in life is to always keep learning and evolving to keep inching closer towards my full potential. I hold the following personal values:

  • Learning
  • Growth and Development
  • Accountability
  • Intellectual Curiosity

I am passionate about all things data science. I enjoy new experiences, gym, bike rides in the city, outdoors/nature, physical activities, listening to music, meditating and handstands.
